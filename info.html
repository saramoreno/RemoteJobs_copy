Front End Web Developer

Posted May 30

Large Foundation

Headquarters: Los Angeles, CA

A large Los Angeles-based Foundation is seeking a Front End Web Developer who:

  • has advanced HTML, CSS/SASS, Javascript/JQuery skills
  • understands Drupal coding standards
  • has experience integrating popular frameworks (such as Foundation and/or Bootstrap) for Drupal 7
  • can demonstrate that s/he writes orderly, commented, Drupal-friendly code
  • has experience dealing w/ diverse media display across all manner of client interfaces
  • understands Drupal's taxonomy and information architecture systems as they relate to theming
  • has a strong eye for design and can deliver pixel perfect results working autonomously as part of a larger remote team
  • pays attention to detail which is reflected in his/her cross-browser, cross-platform, cross-device and compliance testing abilities

The ideal candidate will also possess these professional characteristics:

  • has experience working successfully with a remote team
  • is able to work with minimal supervision and produce visible results
  • completes tasks on time with a high degree of quality
  • is prompt and reliable and has excellent communications skills
  • has a great attitude and is adaptable to change
  • has a sense of humor

    • The project will be 30-40 hours/week for 8-12 weeks (possibly longer) beginning in July 2014. We will have daily scrum calls at 9:30am PST (though this time may change). Daily attendance to these calls will be necessary.
